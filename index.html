🌧️ Monsoon Escapes in the Switzerland of Karnataka

Tucked in the misty hills of the Western Ghats, Sakleshpur — fondly known as the Switzerland of Karnataka — is at its monsoon-best. From cascading streams to mist-covered peaks, Regenta Resort Sakleshpur has been the perfect escape for families, teams, and travelers seeking luxury with nature.

June and July brought us vibrant milestones, joyful memories, and meaningful connections — here’s what we’ve been up to.

✅ JUNE: We Did This

🧘‍♀️ International Yoga Day

We began the month with serenity. Guests and staff gathered on a misty morning for a soulful yoga session, celebrating balance, breath, and well-being amid nature.

🎒 Local School Outreach

As part of our community care initiative, we adopted a nearby government school and donated books and supplies to young students — a step towards empowering rural education.
