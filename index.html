🌧️ Monsoon Escapes in the Switzerland of Karnataka

Sakleshpur, nestled in the Western Ghats and famously called the Switzerland of Karnataka, is in full monsoon bloom — offering lush coffee trails, cascading streams, and serene landscapes. A perfect backdrop for unforgettable stays.

🌿 About Sakleshpur's Coffee

Known for its rich coffee estates, Sakleshpur produces high-quality Arabica and Robusta beans. Guests can enjoy guided estate walks and fresh plantation brews — a true taste of the hills.
