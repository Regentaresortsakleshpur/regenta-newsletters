🌧️ Monsoon Magic in the Switzerland of Karnataka

Nestled in the misty embrace of the Western Ghats, Sakleshpur — often called the Switzerland of Karnataka — offers the perfect monsoon escape. With cloud-kissed plantations, forest trails, and refreshing rains, Regenta Resort Sakleshpur continues to bring comfort, calm, and connection to families, couples, and corporate teams alike.

As we celebrate the milestones of June and embrace the promise of July, we’re delighted to share the highlights of our journey with you.

✅ June 2025: What We Did

🧘‍♀️ International Yoga Day

A soulful celebration in the hills — guests and staff came together for a tranquil sunrise yoga session that fostered mindfulness and inner peace amidst nature.

🎓 School Outreach: Supporting Local Education

As part of our community initiative, we adopted a nearby government school and donated educational supplies — a small but meaningful step toward empowering young learners.

🚴 New: Cycling Experiences

We introduced bicycles for both adults and children, allowing guests to explore the lush resort trails and surrounding coffee estate at their own pace.
