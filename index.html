🌧️ Monsoon Escapes in the Switzerland of Karnataka

Located in the Western Ghats, Sakleshpur — fondly known as the Switzerland of Karnataka — is drenched in lush green beauty during the monsoon. Known for its coffee estates and misty trails, Regenta Resort Sakleshpur is the ideal destination to experience luxury and nature in harmony.

Sakleshpur is celebrated for its premium Arabica and Robusta coffee plantations. During your stay, enjoy plantation walks, coffee tastings, and estate storytelling sessions — all brewed fresh from our backyard.
